Welcome to CleanLinks!

We’ve refreshed the look-and-feel of CleanLinks! Here is a little summary of what CleanLinks does.

Skip redirects

CleanLinks protects your private life and speeds up your internet experience, by skipping redirect pages that track your movements on the internet.

CleanLinks automatically detects redirect pages, by detecting links embedded in their URLs, and directs you directly to the destination page.

Further cleaning

  • The URLs of the pages that you visit can also contain tracking parameters, such as UTM parameters and many more: CleanLinks also removes those.
  • CleanLinks also prevents javascript modifying links as you click them.

See what happened

The CleanLink popup, opened by clicking the toolbar icon, shows the addon’s activity.

There you can investigate which links got modified and how. This is especially useful to investigate broken links or pages. You can also switch off the add-on for that tab from the popup.

Adjust CleanLinks’ behaviour

CleanLinks has a set of rules, that you can modify. This can be done mainly with the buttons at the bottom of the popup:

  • Allow an embedded link, for legitimate use cases such as a login pages
  • Delete an embedded link, to access the intermediate page without the embedded link in the URL
  • Allow once, to open the URL without any cleaning rules applied
  • Allow javascript to modify links on this page
  • Edit rules, to open the advanced rules editor for the selected link.

The popup elements

The CleanLinks popup mainly shows the history of cleaned links, classified by containing page and tagged with request type and type of cleaning.

Filters up top allow you to reduce the number of links displayed, and selecting a link enables the Actions buttons at the bottom, to adjust the rules.

Try it now!

Click the following link, and then open the CleanLinks popup:

https://track.me/?dest=https%3A%2F%2Fddg.gg

This link should take you to the frontpage of DuckDuckGo instead of the (fictional) track.me redirect page.
